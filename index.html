What We Do

Dräcos Software is a sole proprietorship developing games since 1993. We started with pre-internet games developed for dial-up Bulletin Board Systems (Galicticomm), AAA development for retail box products, Facebook-style Actionscript/Flash games, cross-platform mobile games via Adobe AIR, and now our latest endeavour is an HTML5 offering coming soon to the web!
